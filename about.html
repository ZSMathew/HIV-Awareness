Red Ribbon Initiative Background


The Red Ribbon Initiative was established by Abdi Mpongwe,
a passionate medical student at KCMC University, after
identifying a critical gap in HIV awareness and education among
university students. Despite being in institutions of higher
learning, many students continue to engage in risky sexual behaviors,
often due to misinformation or lack of access to accurate, youth-friendly
education on HIV. Driven by a vision to create change,
Abdi founded the Initiative as a platform to educate, empower,
and engage students through interactive and relatable sessions.
The goal is simple yet powerful: to bridge the knowledge gap and
foster a safer, more informed university community.
